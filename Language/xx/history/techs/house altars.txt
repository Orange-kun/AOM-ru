Dabo Gong, the Lord of the Soil and Ground, bestows prosperity upon your gardens, rendering them faster. 

Dabo Gong has always been worshiped as a guardian of a particular area or a city. He is depicted as a serene old man with a long white beard, a yellow robe and a gold hat. People would go to great lengths to worship him, constructing small shrines with his image inside, believing in receiving good health and prosperity.
