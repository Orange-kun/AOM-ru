Chongli, the God of Fire and War, bestows upon you the Heavenly Fire, which renders all technologies researched in the Armory gold-free.

When his son, the water demon Gong Gong, unleashed floods and attempted to seize the throne of Heaven, the proud Chongli, dressed in shiny armor, rose up against him. Locked in a battle, they fought all across Heaven until Gong Gong was defeated.
